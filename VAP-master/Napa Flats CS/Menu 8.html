Services


Full Service

Our staff sets up, serves, and cleans up your catered event. We provide tablecloths, serving platters, appropriate plates, utensils, and napkins.

Delivery Service

Your food will be delivered ready to serve and in disposable containers. Serving utensils will be provided, along with disposable plates, silverware, and napkins.

Pick Up Service

Pick up your order at Napa Flats. Disposable plates, silverware, and napkins will be provided.

Portable Pizza Oven and Gelato Cart

Have our pizza oven come out to your next event. We cook our pizzas fresh on site, in our wood-burning pizza oven. You can also have our gelato cart come out for a refreshing treat. No electricity necessary.

Customized Catering for Large Events

We are happy to work with you to create a custom catering menu and set-up for your large event.
